Daniel Widgren challenges the idea that you can’t build simple web apps in Erlang.
An Erlang developer and creator of Nova, he focuses on proving that modern web development on the BEAM doesn’t have to be heavy, academic, or “too much OTP.”
His message is simple: Erlang web apps aren’t complicated — you’ve just been told they are.
